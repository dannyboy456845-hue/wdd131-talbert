Overview

Purpose

StudentRex is intended to provide fun activities and choices to students and local residents of Rexburg. The site serves as a go-to guide so users can quickly discover things to do in the area, whether they just moved here or have lived here for years.

Audience

The primary audience is students actively attending BYU-Idaho, but the site also targets local residents of Rexburg who are looking for fun activities. Most users will be young adults (18-30) browsing on their phones or laptops.

Dynamic Elements

I will use JavaScript to dynamically filter and sort activity cards by category (Hiking, Adventure, Nightlife, Chill) and by experience level (New Students, Hidden Gems). Filter buttons will show/hide cards without reloading the page. I also plan to use localStorage to remember the user's last selected filter.

Content

Home Page

Welcome to StudentRex! StudentRex is the go-to guide for all BYU-I students and Rexburg locals looking to find fun activities while here. There are a plethora of options, whether you're new in town or a seasoned veteran of the classics like the Tetons!

Activities Page

Filter categories: Hiking, Adventure, Nightlife, Chill
Experience toggles: New Students, Hidden Gems

Cress Creek Nature Trail

Category: Hiking
A peaceful 1.5-mile trail just 25 minutes from campus. Follows a winding creek through lush greenery with benches and picnic tables along the way. Great for a chill roommate outing or FHE activity.
Difficulty: Easy | Cost: Free
Image: cress-creek.jpg

Grand Teton National Park

Category: Adventure
About a 2-hour drive from Rexburg but absolutely worth it. Scenic drives, hiking, kayaking, horseback riding, and wildlife everywhere. Stop through Jackson Hole on the way for food.
Difficulty: Moderate | Cost: ~$35/vehicle
Image: grand-teton.jpg

Fizz Rizz

Category: Nightlife
Rexburg's only mocktail bar and arcade at 32 College Ave. Retro arcade games, billiards, karaoke, and craft mocktails all under $6. Open Mon-Sat, 5 PM to 1 AM. The closest thing to a nightlife scene in Rexburg.
Difficulty: Easy | Cost: $-$$
Image: fizz-rizz.jpg

Fire and Fizz

Category: Chill
A pottery painting studio and custom soda shop at 14 W 1st South. Pick from 200+ pottery pieces and 70+ glazes to create something unique. Great date spot or group hangout. Open Mon-Sat, 11 AM to 10 PM.
Difficulty: Easy | Cost: $-$$
Image: fire-and-fizz.jpg

Porter Park

Category: Chill
Rexburg's oldest and biggest park right downtown. Home to the Idaho Centennial Carousel — one of only 170 antique wooden carousels in the U.S. — a free splash pad in summer, playground, tennis courts, basketball court, and open fields.
Difficulty: Easy | Cost: Free
Image: porter-park.jpg

Wireframes

Home

The home page will have a brief, welcoming description of StudentRex with a hero image and quick links to the activities page. Nothing too complex — just a clean landing page.

Activities

The activities page is where users filter by category and experience level to find things to do around Rexburg. Activity cards will display in a responsive grid layout.

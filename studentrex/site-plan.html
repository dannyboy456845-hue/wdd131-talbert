Overview

Purpose

StudentRex is intended to provide fun activities and choices to students and local residents of Rexburg. That is the inspiration behind the name of this project as well

Audience

Mostly students actively attending BYU-I but also local residents of Rexburg that are looking for fun activites to do.

Dynamic elements

I intend on using filtering and sorting the page in categories based off of the type of activity. I will need Javascript for that. There's going to be a button for clicking to filter too.

Content

Home page

Welcome to StudentRex! StudentRex is the go-to-guide for all BYU-I students and Rexburg locals find fun activities to do while here. There are a plethora of options, whether you're new here in town, or a seasoned veteran of the classics like the Tetons!

Activities

Activities Page Filter categories: Hiking, Adventure, Nightlife, Chill Experience toggles: New Students, Hidden Gems Activity Cards: Cress Creek Nature Trail Category: Hiking A peaceful 1.5-mile trail just 25 minutes from campus. Follows a winding creek through lush greenery with benches and picnic tables along the way. Great for a chill roommate outing or FHE activity. Difficulty: Easy | Cost: Free Image: cress-creek.jpg Grand Teton National Park Category: Adventure About a 2-hour drive from Rexburg but absolutely worth it. Scenic drives, hiking, kayaking, horseback riding, and wildlife everywhere. Stop through Jackson Hole on the way for food. Difficulty: Moderate | Cost: ~$35/vehicle Image: grand-teton.jpg Fizz Rizz Category: Nightlife Rexburg's only mocktail bar and arcade at 32 College Ave. Retro arcade games, billiards, karaoke, and craft mocktails all under $6. Open Mon-Sat, 5 PM to 1 AM. The closest thing to a nightlife scene in Rexburg. Difficulty: Easy | Cost: $-$$ Image: fizz-rizz.jpg Fire and Fizz Category: Chill A pottery painting studio and custom soda shop at 14 W 1st South. Pick from 200+ pottery pieces and 70+ glazes to create something unique. Great date spot or group hangout. Open Mon-Sat, 11 AM to 10 PM. Difficulty: Easy | Cost: $-$$ Image: fire-and-fizz.jpg Porter Park Category: Chill Rexburg's oldest and biggest park right downtown. Home to the Idaho Centennial Carousel — one of only 170 antique wooden carousels in the U.S. — a free splash pad in summer, playground, tennis courts, basketball court, and open fields. Difficulty: Easy | Cost: Free Image: porter-park.jpg
